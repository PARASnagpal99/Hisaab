# Hisaab

## Featurs :
* Helps you managing your expenses in a nice and easy way .
* You can create , update and delete your expenses on various items and keep a track of your spendings and savings .

## Tech Stack :
* NodeJs
* ExpressJs
* MongoDb
* ReactJS
